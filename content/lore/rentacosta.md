## Level 1: Salt-Worn Trade


Rentacosta is a relaxed free city of sailors, traders, borrowed words, and salt-worn
manners. It deals more freely with merfolk than most ports can manage.

Rentacostan speakers often sound worldly without sounding solemn. They know that trade
works best when everyone pretends the bargain was easy.
