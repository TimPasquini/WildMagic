Threen is a wealthy canal-kingdom, officially independent, though everyone knows it answers
to the emperor. It prides itself on literature and fine artisan goods.

## Level 1: Careful Independence

```toml meta
name = "threen_familiar"
description = "Familiar knowledge about Threen, the wealthy canal kingdom that is independent in name and imperial in practice."
triggers = ["literature", "artisan", "writers", "water", "emperor", "shadow", "insult", "careful"]
```

Threen is wealthy, literate, canal-cut, and officially independent. Everyone understands
that the emperor's shadow still falls across the water, but Threen prizes the fiction of
standing alone.

Its artisans and writers enjoy sharp reputations. A Threenian may speak carefully around
imperial power while making sure you hear the insult anyway.
