## Level 1: Bloodline Politics

```toml meta

description = "Familiar knowledge about Monteary horse breeding, guarded stallions, prized geldings, and the politics of bloodlines."
triggers = ["stallion", "gelding", "bloodline", "pasture", "foal", "breeding", "marriage", "bargain"]
```

Monteary breeds the finest horses in the world and protects that reputation like a royal
treasury. Geldings are exported freely and proudly; stallions are watched, traded, or
withheld with political care.

Horse talk in Monteary is never only horse talk. Bloodlines, pasture rights, stolen
breedings, and marriage bargains can all hide under a polite question about a foal.
