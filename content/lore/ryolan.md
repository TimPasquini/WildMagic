## Level 1: Duel Witnesses


The palm-cut that begins a duel is not mere theater; it descends from older rites where
blood made the promise binding.

Ryolani pride can be generous or exhausting. They care who witnessed a vow, who flinched,
and whether a victory was clean enough to sing about.
