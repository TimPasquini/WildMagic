## Level 1: Sacred Cultures

```toml meta

description = "Familiar knowledge about Ontrian yoghurt tribes, clan ritual cultures, and food-borne powers."

```

Ontria is a land of tribes whose sacred yoghurt cultures are family history, ritual tool,
and magic vessel. Eating from the right culture in the right rite grants a clan its
peculiar powers.

Outsiders joke about yoghurt at their peril. To an Ontrian, a culture can be heirloom,
ancestor, oath, and weapon.
