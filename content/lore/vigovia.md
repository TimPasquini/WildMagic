Vigovia proper is the imperial heartland: orderly, proud, legalistic, and fond of paperwork.
Families boast about sons and daughters accepted into charter schools the way others boast
about physicians or judges.

Vigovians tend to see themselves as the adults in the room. Other peoples tend to find them
stiff, beard-proud, permit-drunk, and too sure that every problem has a form for it.
