Charter magic is the Empire's licensed magic: worked by trained charter mages to a fixed,
approved repertoire. Cold, precise, repeatable, and legal.

## Level 1: Licensed Traditions

```toml meta
name = "charter_truth"
description = "Familiar knowledge about how the charter subsumed and licensed old magical traditions."
triggers = ["charter", "tradition", "traditions", "outlaw", "outlawed", "forbidden", "banned", "ban", "licensed"]
```

The charter did not invent magic; it tamed it, licensing safe versions of the old
traditions and outlawing whatever it judged dangerous. A modern woven charm or blessed
tattoo is an old folk art now performed by a licensed mage.
