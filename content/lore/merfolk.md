## Level 1: Ocean Distance

```toml meta
name = "merfolk_familiar"
description = "Familiar knowledge about merfolk pride, ocean isolation, trade distance, and superiority toward land-folk."
triggers = ["trade", "bargain", "warmth", "xenophobic", "land", "landfolk", "deep", "ocean"]
```

Merfolk keep to the deep ocean, proud and standoffish, holding themselves above land-folk
even when trade requires contact. They may bargain, but warmth is rare and often strategic.

Land dwellers tend to call them xenophobic. Merfolk tend to consider that word proof that
land dwellers talk too much about people they do not understand.
