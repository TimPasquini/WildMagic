## Level 1: Holds And Bone Work

```toml meta

description = "Familiar knowledge about Brall, the Bone Jarls, ale-halls, tall tales, scrimshaw, and lawful bone taboos."

```

Brall is a land of holds, ale, scrimshaw, tall stories, and the Bone Jarls. Its old magic
worked through carved whale and beast bone, with craft and ancestry tangled together.

Bralli speakers often treat boasting as hospitality. The charter tolerates some bone work
when licensed, but human bone remains a taboo that can curdle a room.
